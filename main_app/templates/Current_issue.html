Effectiveness of Structured Teaching on CAUTI Prevention Among Nurses

This study evaluates the impact of a structured teaching program on nurses' knowledge of Catheter-Associated Urinary Tract Infection (CAUTI) prevention. Results show significant improvement: pre-test knowledge was poor (37%) to fair (50%), while post-test showed 70% with good knowledge. The program enhanced awareness of hand hygiene, aseptic insertion, and care protocols.

Published: September 2025 | Category: Nursing Education & Infection Control

Knowledge Assessment of Respectful Maternity Care Among Midwives

This study assesses midwives' knowledge of Respectful Maternity Care (RMC) at NMCH, Jamuhar. Results show strong conceptual awareness (87.3% in General Knowledge) but practical challenges in implementation (75.7% in Challenges & Solutions). Higher education levels significantly correlated with better RMC knowledge, highlighting the importance of targeted training.

Published: September 2025 | Category: Maternal Health & Nursing

Data Intelligence Tools in Diabetes Mellitus: Applications and Future Directions

This comprehensive review explores how data intelligence (AI, machine learning) transforms diabetes care across prevention, diagnosis, and management. The study covers applications from glycemic control and complication surveillance to India-specific digital health initiatives, highlighting how these tools enable personalized, proactive diabetes care.

Published: September 2025 | Category: Medical Data Science & Diabetes Care

AI in Drug Discovery and Development: Comprehensive Review

This comprehensive review examines how artificial intelligence transforms pharmaceutical research, from drug discovery to manufacturing. The study covers AI applications in target identification, drug design, toxicity prediction, clinical trials, and pharmaceutical manufacturing, highlighting how AI accelerates development while reducing costs and time.

Published: September 2025 | Category: Pharmaceutical AI & Drug Development
